Contacting Douglas Breault
Doug

The conversation shouldn’t stop here. Every situation is unique and I value your experiences. I invite you to reach out to me directly, for feedback on my articles or to start a dialogue on how we can transform your challenges into opportunities.

Want help or advice?

While I’m always happy to connect, there are a few areas where I cannot be helpful:

  1. Landing your first job or a position at one of the Big Tech companies. My early career path was molded through attending a military academy, serving my communities in a non-military capacity, and engaging directly with communities around the world for charity. My experiences are not likely to be helpful.
  2. Referrals. I take recommendations seriously and will only refer people I have worked with and know are a good fit for the role.
  3. Sponsored content. I write to share my knowledge and learn from others who love engineering, technology, leadership, and community. I was raised on the early internet before banner ads and this blog will hearken back to that spirit of sharing. I will not promote products or services.

How I can help:

  1. Work with you to address specific team or organizational challenges.
  2. Respond to concise questions.
  3. Contribute to your engineering, technology, and leadership publication. I generally avoid podcasts and video format unless they are extraordinarily compelling.
  4. Connect on LinkedIn or Twitter.
